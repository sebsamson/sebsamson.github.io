In 2009, I served as the main Game Designer on Petz Fantasy TM, a two-SKU game for the Nintendo DS, featuring Sunshine Magic and Moonlite Magic. The game offers a collection of 40 magical pets that players can train and care for. The pets' magical powers can be honed through various stylus-based minigames, and players must also cater to their pets' needs for food, hygiene, and play.

Working on the first title of a new brand was a rich experience that taught me a lot about branding and international marketing.

Genre: Nintendo DS-DSi, Ubisoft, Life Simulation, Fantastic, Stylus
