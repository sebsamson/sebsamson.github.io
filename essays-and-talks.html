Video Games and Cybernetics

2008 | Video Games, Cybernetics, Feedback Loop, Emergent Gameplay

This French essay explores the relationship between video games and cybernetics. It discusses how video games can be seen as cybernetic systems, with players and the game interacting and influencing each other in a feedback loop. The essay also delves into the concept of emergent gameplay that arises from these interactions.
