New Media Projects

Here are some of the personal projects I've worked on over the years. These projects span a range of areas, including digital media, game development, web design, and more. Each project was a learning experience and an opportunity to explore new ideas and technologies.

Kilo-Jam

2008 | Interactive Installation

Kilo-Jam was a project that involved creating an interactive installation in a public space. The installation was designed to be playful and engaging, encouraging participants to interact with the installation and with each other. The project was a collaborative effort and was presented at a public event.

Sans Coeur Circuit

2010 | Interactive Installation

Sans Coeur Circuit was an interactive installation that was designed to explore the concept of heartbeats and rhythm. The installation used sensors to detect the heartbeats of participants and translated these into rhythmic sounds, creating a unique and immersive soundscape. The project was presented at a public event.

Projet Xion

2012 | Multimedia Project

Projet Xion was a multimedia project that combined video, sound, and interactive elements to create an immersive experience. The project was designed to be a sensory journey, inviting participants to explore and interact with the installation. The project was presented at a public event.
