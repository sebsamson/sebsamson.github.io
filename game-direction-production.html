Angle

I lead teams from concept through launch and live service. My focus is practical: clear goals, healthy cadence, and decisions that turn ambiguity into a deliverable plan. I partner closely with product, engineering, art, and publishing/platform stakeholders to ship and operate games.

How I Lead

  • Define roadmaps, milestones, and delivery plans that teams can execute confidently.
  • Build sustainable live-ops cadence (events, updates, quality and support workflows).
  • Align cross-functional teams and leadership around scope, trade-offs, and player value.
  • Run feedback loops from playtests and telemetry into prioritised backlogs.
  • Support org health: hiring, mentoring, process improvements, and clarity of ownership.
