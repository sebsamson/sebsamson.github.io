Angle

I lead and support design teams building systems that are fun, readable, and sustainable to operate: progression, rewards, economies, and live cadence. I also offer gamification consultation for products outside games (NDA-friendly), focused on motivation, learning, and long-term engagement without dark patterns.

What I Deliver

  • Game/system vision that translates into implementable specs and backlogs.
  • Progression and economy audits with concrete improvement plans.
  • Engagement loop tuning (onboarding, goals, challenges, rewards, cadence).
  • Playtest and telemetry feedback loops (hypotheses, success criteria, rollout).
  • Cross-functional alignment: design that ships, scales, and is understandable to teams.

Gamification Consultation (NDA)

I’ve supported non-disclosed work across health, learning, and platform experiences. Typical engagements include:

  • Health & wellbeing apps: habit loops, streaks, progression, and sustainable rewards.
  • Learning apps & platforms: mastery paths, challenges, pacing, and feedback clarity.
  • Creator/platform products: onboarding, long-term retention, and incentive alignment.
  • Ethical engagement: avoid exploitative patterns while still supporting business goals.
